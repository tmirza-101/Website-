Our Mission

The North American Mobeds Council Education Portal is dedicated to providing comprehensive, accessible, and high-quality religious education to Zoroastrians across North America and beyond. Our platform serves as a digital university for Zoroastrian studies, connecting students with experienced Mobeds and scholars.
